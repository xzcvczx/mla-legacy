Development Debugging Information

This page displays the stored preference values and configuration values for your meter demo. You can modify this page to display other application values to assist with application debugging.

After you activate your meter, you can view the authentication POST content that was received from Google on your Google PowerMeter gadget's settings page. During activation, this information was parsed and loaded into an instance of the POWER_METER_PREFERENCES structure called gPowerMeterPreferences.
